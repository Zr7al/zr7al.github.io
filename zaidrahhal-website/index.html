FAQ

Common Questions

Ask Something Else

How long does a project take?

Most websites are completed within 2–4 weeks depending on scope. A focused landing page can be ready in under a week. I'll give you a clear timeline at the start of every project.

What platform do you use?

I build with clean, custom HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no bloated page builders. For clients who need a CMS, I also work with WordPress. Custom code means your site is faster, more secure, and 100% yours.

Who writes the copy?

You provide the core information about your business and I'll structure and refine it into clear, professional copy. For clients who need full copywriting, I can recommend trusted writers.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes. Monthly maintenance from $50/month — security updates, minor edits, performance checks, and priority support. Most clients opt in for peace of mind.

Do you handle hosting?

Yes. I'll set up hosting on reliable platforms and handle all technical deployment. You own the account — I take care of the setup so you don't have to think about it.

Can you redesign my existing site?

Absolutely. I'll modernise the design, improve structure and speed, and migrate carefully — keeping your SEO rankings intact throughout.

Do you build Arabic (RTL) websites?

Yes. I build fully RTL Arabic and bilingual Arabic/English sites — designed from the ground up, not just a flipped layout.

Do you offer SEO?

Yes — every site includes on-page SEO: optimised meta tags, clean structure, fast load times, and Google Search Console setup. Deeper SEO campaigns available as a standalone service.
